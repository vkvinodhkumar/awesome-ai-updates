## Terminology

*   **Long-Horizon Models:** AI systems designed to work on complex tasks that take a long time to complete, requiring the model to maintain focus and context over many steps.
*   **Physical AI:** The integration of AI into physical hardware (like robots) to help them perceive, learn, and interact with the physical world.
*   **Return on Compute:** A metric measuring how much useful work is produced relative to the amount of computing power (and money) consumed.
*   **Model Evaluation:** The process of testing an AI model before it is released to ensure it is safe, accurate, and behaves as expected.
*   **Autonomous Agents:** AI software that can perform tasks on its own, such as using other software tools or navigating websites, without needing the user to give every single command.
