## Terminology

- **Red-teaming:** The practice of rigorously testing a system to find security vulnerabilities or flaws.
- **Model Routing:** A system that directs a specific prompt to the most efficient model based on pre-defined criteria like cost or speed.
- **Fine-tuning:** The process of taking a pre-trained model and training it further on a specific dataset to improve performance on specialized tasks.
- **AI Agents:** AI systems designed to perform autonomous tasks and interact with external tools or environments.
- **Multimodal:** AI models capable of processing and generating multiple types of data, such as text, images, and video.
- **Token:** The basic unit of text (often a word or part of a word) that an AI model processes.
